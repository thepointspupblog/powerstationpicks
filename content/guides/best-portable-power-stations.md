## How to Choose

**Capacity needs:**
- Phones and small devices: 300Wh
- Laptops, cameras, drones: 500-1000Wh
- Mini fridge, CPAP, extended trips: 1000-2000Wh
- Full fridge, home backup: 2000Wh+

**Battery type:**
- LiFePO4: 3000-4000 cycles, safer, heavier
- Li-ion: 500-1000 cycles, lighter, cheaper
