## Home Backup Power Math

**Basic essentials for 24 hours:**
- Fridge (100W avg): 2400Wh
- Lights: 240Wh
- Phone charging: 100Wh
- Router: 360Wh
- **Total: ~3100Wh**

This is why 2000Wh+ units are recommended.
